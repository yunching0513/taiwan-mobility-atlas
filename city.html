03 — Vision Zero · 達標軌跡03 — Vision Zero Trajectory

2024 → 2035 → 2050 · Required Trajectory + BAU Projection

以 2024 年實績為基準。亮綠虛線為達成「2035 減半、2050 A1 歸零 / A2 −90%」目標所需軌跡；同色淡色帶為「不積極作為情境」（BAU, business-as-usual）推估 — 採穩健回歸（Theil-Sen 中位數斜率）對近 7 年（2019–2025）建模，避免 COVID 等單一異常年扭曲趨勢方向；下降趨勢用等比衰減避免「誤判為自然歸零」，上升趨勢用線性避免指數爆炸，並錨定於 2024 基準。A2 另設「層級位移偵測」：若整體趨勢顯示大幅成長、但近 3 年實際持平／下降（多為通報口徑變動造成的階梯，非真實激增），則改以近期實況外推。A1 死亡之 HIGH 上界加入「政策停滯」情境：假設政府不積極作為、僅靠車輛安全與醫療進步等被動因素，每年至多自然下降 1%，避免低估政策缺口。不積極作為情境與目標軌跡之間的差距，即為政策必須補足的努力量。From 2024 baseline. Lime dashed = trajectory required to meet 2035 (−50%) and 2050 (A1 = 0 / A2 = −90%) targets. Tinted band (same hue as the metric) = business-as-usual projection — robust Theil-Sen regression (median pairwise slope) on the last 7 years (2019–2025), so a single anomalous year like COVID-2020 can't flip the projected direction; log-linear for declining series (asymptotes, never spuriously hits zero), linear for growing series (no exponential blow-up), anchored at the 2024 baseline. A2 also gets a level-shift guard: if the full window shows strong growth but the last 3 years are flat/declining (usually a reporting-coverage step, not a real surge), the current regime is projected instead. For A1 the HIGH bound is widened to a policy-stagnation floor of at most 1%/year passive decline (vehicle safety, trauma medicine) absent active intervention, so the band doesn't understate the policy gap. The gap between BAU and the required path is the policy effort needed.

07 — Notes 資料說明07 — Notes
關於本地圖About this map

本地圖整理自內政部警政署 A1 級道路交通事故公開資料， 篩選發生於該縣市之事故，年度範圍為民國 105 至 114 年（2016–2025）。 A1 為「造成人員當場或 24 小時內死亡」之事故等級，每筆事件可能涉及多位當事人。

Compiled from the National Police Agency A1 (fatal) road-crash open dataset, filtered to crashes in this county, covering 2016–2025. A1 = a crash where someone died on the spot or within 24 hours; each event may involve several parties.

不同年度的資料完整度不同：2016、2017、2021 為「簡式」欄位 （僅含時間、地點、傷亡、車種），2018、2019、2020、2022 — 2025 為完整欄位 （含天候、光線、道路類別、肇因等）；2016 與 2017 完全無經緯度資訊， 故地圖僅顯示 2018 年後之事件，但所有年份皆納入趨勢與時間／月份分布之統計。

Field completeness varies by year: 2016, 2017, 2021 use a simplified schema (time, place, casualties, vehicle only); 2018–2020, 2022–2025 are full (weather, lighting, road class, cause, …). 2016 & 2017 have no coordinates, so the map shows 2018+ events only — but all years count in the trend and time/month sections.

「運具類別」採用受害者邏輯：每筆事件以其中最脆弱用路人之運具為類別 （優先序：行人 > 自行車/慢車 > 機車 > 汽/貨車）。這與 A1 原始資料的「當事者順位 P1」不同 —— 左轉小客車撞死行人之案件，P1 是駕駛、運具為汽車，但本地圖會把該事件歸類為「行人」。 此調整反映「誰真正在道路上喪生」之問題，與政策關切的「脆弱用路人保護」一致。 原始 P1 分類保留於每筆事件的 principal_mode 欄位。

Victim-based mode: each event is classified by its most-vulnerable road user (priority: pedestrian > bicycle/slow vehicle > motorcycle > car/truck) — unlike the raw "party-1" field. A left-turning car that kills a pedestrian has the driver (a car) as party-1 in the raw data, but is classified here as "pedestrian." This answers "who actually dies on the road," matching the policy focus on protecting vulnerable users. The original party-1 class is kept in each event's principal_mode field.

幹線道路圖層：地圖上的紅／橙／藍線為國道、快速公路、省道， 資料來源為交通部公路局 ROAD_國省道(含快速公路)_1150409 圖資 （TWD97/TM2 經緯度轉換、Douglas-Peucker 15m 簡化）。可在地圖上方「幹線 Hwys」按鈕切換顯示與隱藏。

Highway overlay: red/orange/blue lines are national freeways, expressways and provincial highways, from the MOTC Highway Bureau ROAD_國省道(含快速公路)_1150409 dataset (TWD97/TM2 → WGS84, Douglas-Peucker 15 m). Toggle via the "幹線 Hwys" buttons above the map.

A1 = Fatal crash (death within 24 h). Older years (2016–2017, 2021) use a simplified schema. Coordinates absent for 2016–2017.
